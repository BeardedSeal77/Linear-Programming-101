# Supply Selection Problem

Captain America needs to take some supplies to one of the Avenger bases. However, as the transport ship is being loaded, he discovers that there is limited room for the precious cargo and he must now decide which piece of supplies to take. The table below displays the available supplies to take with along with their relative space requirement and how much their worth is. The transport ship hold can only take 40 tons of supplies and only one of each supply is currently available.

## Available Supplies

| Supply | Weight | Worth |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Medical supplies | 11 | 2 |
| Ammunition | 8 | 3 |
| Food rations | 6 | 3 |
| Weapons | 14 | 5 |
| Water | 10 | 2 |
| Tents | 10 | 4 |
